About

I live in San Diego, and I'm homeschooled, a junior in Highschool. I started coding several years back, learning python, then javascript. I eventually discovered Unity, a powerful Game Engine that uses C# and allows you to publish games to many platforms. I was able to publish games to the Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and the web. My younger brother dreams up all sorts of game ideas and I code them up. He also helps out with Level Design, some art, and recently has done some soundtracks in GarageBand. Recently, I've been learning Android Studio, building a free, open-source budgetting app. I encourage you to check out some of our work below!
